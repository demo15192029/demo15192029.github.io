All forms of life shouldbe treated equally and not born to be exploited as mere food. Human interference in the natural order has caused multiple impacts on other living beings. Human civilization is playing the role of a dictator on Earth, being both selfish and arrogant. The game developers aim to convey the interconnectedness and commonality of life through this game, emphasizing that all life forms deserve respect and urging people to value the interdependence and mutual respect among different forms of life. It serves as a reminder to cherish the rights of all living beings and don't be a troublemaker in the ecosystem!

With our work, let us unite in understanding the value of every life and work towards
a harmonious coexistence with all living beings on our planet.
